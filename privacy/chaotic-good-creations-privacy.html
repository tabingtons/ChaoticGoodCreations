Chaotic Good Creations' Website Privacy Policy

How we handle your information — clearly, securely, and respectfully.


1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to share with us, including:

  • Contact form submissions: If you send us a message through our website, we collect your name, email address, and any information you include in your message. These messages are sent directly to our email inbox via Formspree and are not stored in any online dashboard or database.
  • Booking information: When you schedule a meeting through Cal.com, your name, email, and appointment details are collected so we can confirm and manage your booking. Cal.com may store this information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

We do not use analytics tools, cookies, or any tracking technologies on this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for purposes directly related to your enquiry or booking, such as:

  • Responding to messages you send via our contact form.
  • Managing and confirming discovery sessions booked through Cal.com.
  • Following up on projects or services you’ve expressed interest in.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Contact form submissions are delivered to our secure email account and not stored elsewhere. Booking information is handled by Cal.com, which uses secure servers and encryption. We take reasonable steps to protect all personal information against loss, unauthorised access, and misuse.

4. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete that information.

To make a request, contact us at hello@chaoticgoodcreations.co.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate parts of our site:

  • Formspree (for contact form submissions)
  • Cal.com (for booking discovery sessions)

These providers have their own privacy policies that explain how they handle your data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised date at the top.
