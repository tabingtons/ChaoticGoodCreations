Dig Deeper

Learn to Effectively Name How You Feel

Dig Deeper helps you identify and understand your emotions using the Feelings Wheel. Expand your emotional vocabulary, grow your self-awareness, and become more present in everyday life.

A Simple Tool for a Complex World

Dig Deeper simplifies emotional intelligence into an easy, meaningful journaling experience.

Easily Identify & Record

Quickly find the right word for how you feel using the interactive wheel. Record your emotions in seconds and reflect on past entries to uncover patterns and personal growth.

Take Note of the 'Why'

Understanding why you feel a certain way is key. Add a short note to capture what happened and the thoughts behind each emotion.

Explore and Dig Deeper

Go beyond basic feelings like 'Happy' or 'Angry'. Dig Deeper's Feelings Wheel guides you to more nuanced emotions like 'Confident', 'Furious', or 'Hesitant' for a richer understanding of yourself.
