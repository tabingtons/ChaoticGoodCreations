Ako Playrooms

Shopify Design & Development for a Creative Children's Brand

A full website refresh and Shopify store build that helps Ako Playrooms showcase their playroom design services and sell directly to customers across New Zealand.

View Live Site →

Project Overview

Ako Playrooms are a New Zealand–based studio designing inspiring children's play spaces for homes and businesses. They approached Chaotic Good Creations looking for a website that could better reflect their brand, attract new design clients, and launch their new e-commerce offering.

Their previous site, built on Squarespace, wasn't structured to drive enquiries or handle online sales. Together we planned a full redesign that balanced storytelling, functionality, and conversion.

The Challenge

The key challenge was blending two distinct goals within one site:

  • Positioning Ako Playrooms as trusted interior playroom designers, and
  • Creating a seamless online store experience for direct-to-customer product sales.

Each audience needed clear pathways — service-based visitors toward consultation, and retail customers toward the shop — without either experience competing for attention.

Our Approach

We migrated the site from Squarespace to Shopify, giving Ako Playrooms a scalable platform for both service promotion and online sales. Using their established brand colours and playful design language, I rebuilt the information architecture to guide visitors smoothly from inspiration to action.

Through workshops and milestone feedback loops, we refined layout flow, SEO-driven page structure, and mobile responsiveness. The design focuses on warmth, simplicity, and a calm sense of creativity that reflects Ako Playrooms' physical spaces.

Collaboration & Process

This project was a close collaboration between Chaotic Good Creations and the Ako Playrooms team. They provided most of the copywriting and insight into their target audience, while I focused on layout strategy, UX, and technical implementation.

We held milestone workshops at key stages — from initial wireframes through to testing — ensuring every decision supported both their business goals and their customer journey.

Outcome

The redesigned website launched in August '25, with the new Shopify store following in November '25. Early feedback from Ako Playrooms has been highly positive, and the new structure now gives them a clear foundation for marketing and growth.
